Gloria Jean's

Project by Creed Hospitality in partnership with Afrillence | Launched October 2020

Follow

@gloria_jeans_on_florida

Project brief :

Setting up a 30 seater, quick service coffee and chiller house franchise located in Durban

Scope

Human Resources

  • Hiring & Recruitment of a staff compliment of 10
  • Advise and negotiate salaries for all staff members based on industry standard and experience
  • 6 day intense training of front of house and back of house staff, paying particular attention to service, attitude, phycology of hospitality, conflict management, customer relations and growth within the industry using our tailor made Creed Hospitality Training Manual
  • Create a salary schedule that computes days worked and amounts due at the end of each month

Menu Development

  • Create the food menu,
  • Costing and pricing of the food menu in line with generally acceptable margins to ensure maximum return
  • Sourcing and designing the physical menu boards
  • Computing and placing the opening food order
  • Complete an kitchen bible that is bound and presented which details recipe costings against pricing to determine GP%
  • Breakdown the restaurant menu into a training menu that highlights key definitions and methods of preparation for easy understanding for waiters and kitchen staff
  • Menu tasting and plating recommendations and changes

Stock Reporting ,Accounting and Monthly Financials

  • Compute accurate stock sheets
  • Compute prep sheets for kitchen
  • Compute a creditors reconciliation sheet for weekly supplier payments
  • Compute and Income Statement template for weekly/ monthly reporting of financial activity
  • Compute a cash reconciliation sheet for the manager to account for any cash movement within the space

Suppliers

  • Recommend suppliers for all products used in the restaurant
  • Facilitate the opening of supplier accounts and negotiate payment terms
  • Set up the Central Kitchen model of supply of food items and pastries

Launch Event

  • Curate the launch menu
  • Organise, plan and execute the entire event
