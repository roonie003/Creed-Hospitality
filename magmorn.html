Margaret Mornings

Project by Creed Hospitality| October 2020

@margaret_mornings

Project brief :

To set up an on- the- go coffee shop with limited seating inside the ICS ( authorised Apple reseller )

Scope:

Human Resources

  • Hiring & Recruitment of a barista
  • Advise and negotiate salaries for all staff members based on industry standard and experience
  • Uniform design and sourcing

Menu Development

  • Develop from scratch the cafe food and drinks menu according to brief
  • Costing and pricing of the food and drinks menu in line with generally acceptable margins to ensure maximum return
  • Sourcing and designing the physical menu
  • Menu tasting and plating recommendations and changes
  • Computing and placing the opening order

Facilities Set up & design

  • Advising owner on final finishing of the restaurant ambience vs functionality
  • Designing the most effective and efficient seating plan for the space
  • Compilation of all service stock ie cutlery, crockery, glassware, kitchen and bar smalls according to the menu and size of restaurant
  • Sourcing of all service stock ie kitchen smalls, glassware, cutlery, crockery, bar smalls and equipment according to the menu

Stock Reporting ,Accounting and Monthly Financials

  • Compute and Income Statement template for weekly/ monthly reporting of financial activity
  • Compute a cash reconciliation sheet for the manager to account for any cash movement within the space

Suppliers

  • Facilitate the opening of supplier accounts and negotiate payment terms
