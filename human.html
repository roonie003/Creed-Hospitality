Our Services

Human Rescources

Without the people there is no business is a mantra that we live by at Creed. The human resources aspect of the business is one (if not the most ) important aspect of the business. Good employees, make life easier for owners as they treat the business as if it were their own. Careful consideration must be taken when recruiting, selecting and hiring staff as the team has the ability to make or break the business. We believe that training is not a onetime event but a continuous process of learning and improvement

Key Services

  • Advertise available positions
  • Conduct interviews and offer a technical line of questioning in interviews
  • Shortlist successful candidates and setting up second interviews were necessary
  • Facilitate and conduct practical interviews with kitchen staff
  • Train staff on all levels using our in-house training manuals for 10 days
  • Negotiate fair salaries for all staff members
  • Prepare and present offer letters and contracts
  • Employee evaluation post probation period
