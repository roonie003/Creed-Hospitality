Financial Projections & Feasibility Analysis

The ultimate goal of any business is to turn a profit. As such it is crucial to have to conduct comprehensive and detailed financial projections for any project beforehand in order for us to assess breakeven points, daily, weekly and monthly targets, reak-even analysis tells you how many units of a product must be sold to cover the fixed and variable costs of production. The break-even point is considered a measure of the margin of safety. Break-even analysis is used broadly, from stock and options trading to corporate budgeting for various projects.
